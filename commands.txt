Local Command to get all candles for a specific Currency in a specific timeframe:
curl "http://127.0.0.1:5000/stocks?crypto=BTC&timeframe=daily" 

Local Command to get all candles in a date range for a specific Currency in a specific timeframe:
curl "http://127.0.0.1:5000/stocks?crypto=BTC&timeframe=daily&start_date=2024-12-01&end_date=2024-12-10"
